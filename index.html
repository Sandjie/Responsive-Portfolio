About Me

My name is Sandra Swiatkiewicz. I was born in Warsaw, Poland and immigrated to the United States when I was just 3 years old. I have lived in New York & New Jersey a majority of my life and just recently moved to Florida 2 years ago. I have been in healthcare for the past 10 years and have decided on a major career change. I am excited to start a new journey. I love learning new and challenging things and I believe that it grows you as a person, both mentally and spiritually. It can be a very humbling experience.

I am looking forward to starting my career as a web developer. I strive to learn as much as possible on this new journey, to become the best that I can be in order to excel my employers and clients needs and expectations.
